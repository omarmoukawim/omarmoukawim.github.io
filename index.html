About Me

Who am I?

Greetings! I am Omar Moukawim, a fervent Control Systems Engineering Master's student in my final year. Rooted in Italy, my journey through the captivating landscape of ICT has been an exciting ride. With a blend of knowledge encompassing Robotics, Control, Transmission Systems, and Telecommunication Networks, I'm driven by an insatiable curiosity for technology and innovation. My expedition spans from amplifying Agile SW testing at Volvo Cars to pioneering cutting-edge cybersecurity solutions for automobiles at Leonardo. This latter endeavor allows me to collaborate with my university and Leonardo Spa to introduce intrusion analysis into vehicle fleets through the development of a novel technology, securing the entire ecosystem from onboard systems to remote infrastructures used for management and updates. Each endeavor fuels my enthusiasm to craft ingenious solutions that shape a brighter tomorrow.
